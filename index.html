Tune Blobs Help

Welcome to the Tune Blobs! Here's how to use it:

Basic Controls

  • DRAG blobs up/down to change their pitch
  • CLICK blobs to select them (see details in panel)
  • PLAY/STOP button toggles auto-play mode
  • RECORD captures your performance
  • SAVE stores your recordings

Blob Types

  • Soprano (Pink) - High pitch range
  • Alto (Yellow) - Medium-high pitch
  • Tenor (Green) - Medium-low pitch
  • Bass (Blue) - Low pitch range

Physics Controls

Adjust these settings to change how the blobs move:

  • Gravity - How strongly blobs fall downward
  • Bounce - How much they bounce off walls
  • Friction - How quickly they slow down
  • Speed - Overall movement speed

Have fun creating your Tune Blobs!
